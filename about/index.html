About Nexus Web Tools

Nexus Web Tools is a growing hub of free, accurate, browser‑based calculators and utilities. We provide instant results with no sign‑up, no paywall, and a privacy‑first approach – all calculations run locally in your browser.

Who We Are

Nexus Web Tools was founded by an Australian technology team with over a decade of experience in software development, data analysis, and financial modelling. After years of working with enterprise-grade financial systems and construction estimation software, Nexus Web Tools recognised that most online calculators were either oversimplified, riddled with ads, or locked behind paywalls. Nexus Web Tools was built to change that — combining rigorous, real‑world formulas with a clean, fast, mobile‑first interface.

Our Expertise

  • Financial modelling: Our compound interest and mortgage calculators use industry‑standard amortisation formulas, including support for LMI/PMI, annual loan fees, and one‑off purchase costs — factors that most free tools ignore.
  • Engineering & construction: Our sister site, Concrete Calculator, handles volume estimation, bag counts, mix ratios, and saw‑cut spacing using Australian Standards (AS 3600) as a reference baseline.
  • Data science: The S‑Curve cash‑flow model implements a logistic growth function with configurable start/end values, period‑by‑period breakdowns, and Excel export — designed for project managers and financial analysts.
  • Software engineering: All tools are built with vanilla JavaScript, run entirely client‑side, and are deployed via GitHub Pages for maximum transparency and uptime.
