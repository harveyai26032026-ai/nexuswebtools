Bond Laddering

By using the bond calculator to ladder your bonds, you can earn more interest over time. For example, if you invest $10,000 in a 5-year bond with an interest rate of 3%, you will earn $1,500 in interest over the 5 years. If you then use the calculator to ladder your bonds by investing the interest earned each year into a new 5-year bond with an interest rate of 3%, you can earn an additional $750 in interest over the next 5 years, for a total of $2,250. This can help you reach your financial goals faster.
