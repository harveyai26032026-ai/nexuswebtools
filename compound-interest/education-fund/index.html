Applications

Example Calculation for Multiple Children

For example, if you have two children, ages 5 and 8, and you want to save $10,000 each for their college education, you'll need to save $20,000 total. With a 5% interest rate, it will take approximately 17 years to reach your goal if you start saving now. However, if you wait until your children are 10 and 13 years old, it will take over 20 years to reach your goal.

Planning for Special Education Needs

The education fund calculator can also help you plan for special education needs, such as therapy sessions or personalized tutoring. Please provide details on the specific services required and their estimated cost to get an accurate estimate.

Accounting for Inflation

The education fund calculator takes into account the effects of inflation on the estimated cost of education. This means that as time passes and your child grows older, the cost of education is expected to increase due to inflation. By accounting for this, you can better plan and prepare for the future costs of educating your child.

Examples of How to Use the Calculator for Multiple Children

For example, if you have two children, one entering kindergarten and the other entering 9th grade, you can use the calculator to estimate the total cost of their educations over the next 18 years. You can also adjust the calculator's estimates based on your children's desired majors or fields of study.
