Background

Convert cups (cup) to millilitres (ml) instantly. This is one of the most commonly needed conversions in the volume category, required in engineering, science, and everyday life across different measurement systems.

Understanding cup and ml

Cups and millilitres are both units of volume. cup is part of the imperial system, while ml belongs to the metric system. The exact conversion factor is 236.588 (0.00422675 in reverse).

When You Need This Conversion

This conversion is needed when working with international specifications, reading metric measurements in a imperial country, or using equipment calibrated in different units.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert cups to ml?

US: multiply by 236.588. Metric: multiply by 250. 2 US cups = 473.2 ml.

What is an Australian metric cup?

250 ml. Different from US cup (236.6 ml). Always check which standard.

How many cups in 500 ml?

500 ÷ 236.588 = 2.11 US cups, or 2 metric cups exactly.
