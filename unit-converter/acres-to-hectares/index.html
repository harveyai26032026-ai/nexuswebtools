Background

Convert acres (ac) to hectares (ha) instantly. This is one of the most commonly needed conversions in the area category, required in engineering, science, and everyday life across different measurement systems.

Understanding ac and ha

Acres and hectares are both units of area. ac is part of the metric system, while ha belongs to the imperial system. The exact conversion factor is 0.404686 (2.47105 in reverse).

When You Need This Conversion

This conversion is needed when working with international specifications, reading imperial measurements in a metric country, or using equipment calibrated in different units.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert acres to hectares?

Multiply by 0.404686. 10 acres = 4.05 ha.

How many acres in a hectare?

1 ha = 2.47105 acres. Quick: ~2.5 acres.

What is a quarter-acre block in hectares?

0.25 acres = 0.1012 ha. The iconic Australian 'quarter-acre block'.
