Background

Weight (technically mass) conversion is essential in cooking, health, shipping, science, and international trade. The kilogram is the SI base unit of mass, defined since 2019 by the Planck constant. The pound is defined as exactly 0.45359237 kilograms. Despite the precision of these definitions, everyday weight conversion remains a common need due to the coexistence of metric and imperial systems.

Weight vs Mass — A Technical Distinction

In everyday language, weight and mass are used interchangeably. Scientifically, mass is the amount of matter in an object (kilograms), while weight is the force gravity exerts on that mass (newtons). For all practical conversion purposes, we convert between mass units — kg, lbs, oz, etc.

The Stone — A British Unit

The stone (14 pounds or 6.35 kg) is used almost exclusively in the UK and Ireland for expressing human body weight. It is not used in the US (where body weight is in pounds) or in metric countries (where body weight is in kilograms).

Applications

Cooking and Recipes

Recipes from different countries use different units. An American recipe might call for 16 oz of flour, while an Australian one uses 450 g. Accurate conversion is essential for baking, where precision matters.

Health and Fitness

Tracking body weight across different measurement systems. Convert your weight from kg to lbs or stone depending on where you live and which system your health app or doctor uses.

Shipping and Logistics

International shipping rates are often quoted per kilogram or per pound. Converting between the two is necessary for calculating costs and comparing carriers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert kg to lbs?

Multiply kilograms by 2.20462. For example, 70 kg × 2.20462 = 154.32 lbs. A quick approximation: double the kg and add 10% — 70 kg ≈ 140 + 14 = 154 lbs.

How many kilograms are in a stone?

1 stone equals 6.35029 kilograms, or exactly 14 pounds. The stone is commonly used in the UK and Ireland for body weight. A 12-stone person weighs approximately 76.2 kg or 168 lbs.

How do you convert ounces to grams?

Multiply ounces by 28.3495. For example, 8 oz × 28.3495 = 226.8 g. For fluid ounces (volume), this is a different conversion — see our volume converter.
