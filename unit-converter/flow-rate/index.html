Background

Flow rate = volume/time. L/s and m³/hr (metric), GPM (US), CFM (HVAC air flow).

Plumbing and Water Supply

Residential: 0.3–0.5 L/s per fixture. Shower: 0.15–0.2 L/s (9–12 L/min).

HVAC Air Flow

Residential AC: 400–800 CFM. 1 CFM = 0.4719 L/s.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert L/s to GPM?

Multiply by 15.8503. 2 L/s = 31.7 GPM.

How do you convert GPM to L/min?

Multiply by 3.7854. 10 GPM = 37.85 L/min.

How do you convert CFM to L/s?

Multiply by 0.4719. 500 CFM = 236.0 L/s.
