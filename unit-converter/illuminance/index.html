Background

Illuminance = light on a surface. Lux (lm/m²) is SI, used internationally. Foot-candles (lm/ft²) used in the US. 1 fc = 10.764 lux.

Recommended Levels

Office: 300–500 lux. Retail: 300–1,500 lux. Warehouse: 100–200 lux. Parking: 20–50 lux.

Lux vs Foot-Candles

1 fc = 10.764 lux. 50 fc ≈ 538 lux. Conversion based on m² vs ft².

Applications

Workplace Compliance

Photography

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert fc to lux?

Multiply by 10.764. 50 fc = 538.2 lux.

How do you convert lux to fc?

Divide by 10.764. 500 lux = 46.5 fc.

Good office lighting level?

300–500 lux (28–46 fc). Detailed tasks: 750–1,000 lux.
