Background

Convert square metres (m²) to square feet (ft²) instantly. This is one of the most commonly needed conversions in the area category, required in engineering, science, and everyday life across different measurement systems.

Understanding m² and ft²

Square metres and square feet are both units of area. m² is part of the imperial system, while ft² belongs to the metric system. The exact conversion factor is 10.7639 (0.092903 in reverse).

When You Need This Conversion

This conversion is needed when working with international specifications, reading metric measurements in a imperial country, or using equipment calibrated in different units.
