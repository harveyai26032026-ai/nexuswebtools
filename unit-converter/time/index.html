Background

Time uses base-60 (sec→min), base-24 (hr→day), and irregular months. These irregularities make manual conversion error-prone, especially for payroll and project planning.

Calendar Irregularity

Months: 28–31 days. Years: 365 (366 leap). Finance uses 30/360, actual/365, or actual/actual conventions.

Decimal Time in Payroll

7 hr 30 min = 7.5 decimal hours. Common source of payroll errors if done manually.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert minutes to decimal hours?

Divide by 60. 45 min = 0.75 hr. So 2 hr 45 min = 2.75 decimal hours.

How many seconds in a day?

86,400 seconds (24 × 60 × 60).

How many working days in a year?

~260 (52 weeks × 5 days) minus ~10–12 public holidays = 248–250 working days in Australia.
