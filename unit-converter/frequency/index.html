Background

Frequency measures cycles per second. Hz is the SI unit. kHz (10³), MHz (10⁶), GHz (10⁹). RPM for rotating machinery.

Electronics and Computing

CPU speeds in GHz (3.5 GHz = 3.5B cycles/s). Radio: kHz (AM) to GHz (Wi-Fi, 5G).

Audio Frequency

Human hearing: 20 Hz – 20 kHz. A4 = 440 Hz, middle C = 261.6 Hz.

Applications

Electronics and Telecom

Wi-Fi: 2.4/5 GHz. 5G: 600 MHz – 39 GHz. Converting is essential for wireless equipment.

Audio and Music

CD quality: 44.1 kHz sampling. EQ frequencies in Hz/kHz.

Machinery and Motors

3,000 RPM = 50 Hz. Grid: 50 Hz (AU) / 60 Hz (US).

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert Hz to kHz?

Divide by 1,000. 5,000 Hz = 5 kHz.

How do you convert RPM to Hz?

Divide by 60. 3,600 RPM = 60 Hz.

What frequency is Wi-Fi?

2.4 GHz and 5 GHz. 5 GHz is faster but shorter range.
