Background

Convert kilowatts (kW) to horsepower (HP) instantly. This is one of the most commonly needed conversions in the power category, required in engineering, science, and everyday life across different measurement systems.

Understanding kW and HP

Kilowatts and horsepower are both units of power. kW is part of the imperial system, while HP belongs to the metric system. The exact conversion factor is 1.34102 (0.7457 in reverse).

When You Need This Conversion

This conversion is needed when working with international specifications, reading metric measurements in a imperial country, or using equipment calibrated in different units.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert kW to HP?

Multiply by 1.34102. 100 kW = 134.1 HP.

How do you convert HP to kW?

Multiply by 0.7457. 200 HP = 149.1 kW.

Metric vs mechanical HP?

Mechanical: 745.7 W. Metric (PS): 735.5 W. 1 HP = 1.0139 PS.
