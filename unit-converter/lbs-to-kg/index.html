Background

Convert pounds (lbs) to kilograms (kg) instantly. This is one of the most commonly needed conversions in the weight category, required in engineering, science, and everyday life across different measurement systems.

Understanding lbs and kg

Pounds and kilograms are both units of weight. lbs is part of the metric system, while kg belongs to the imperial system. The exact conversion factor is 0.453592 (2.20462 in reverse).

When You Need This Conversion

This conversion is needed when working with international specifications, reading imperial measurements in a metric country, or using equipment calibrated in different units.
