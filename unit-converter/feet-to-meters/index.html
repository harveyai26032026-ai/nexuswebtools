Background

Convert feet (ft) to metres (m) instantly. This is one of the most commonly needed conversions in the length category, required in engineering, science, and everyday life across different measurement systems.

Understanding ft and m

Feet and metres are both units of length. ft is part of the metric system, while m belongs to the imperial system. The exact conversion factor is 0.3048 (3.28084 in reverse).

When You Need This Conversion

This conversion is needed when working with international specifications, reading imperial measurements in a metric country, or using equipment calibrated in different units.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert feet to metres?

Multiply by 0.3048. 10 ft = 3.048 m.

What is 6 feet in metres?

6 × 0.3048 = 1.829 m (height of a tall person).

How many feet in a metre?

1 m = 3.28084 ft.
