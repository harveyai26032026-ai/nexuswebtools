Background

Convert millimetres (mm) to inches (in) instantly. This is one of the most commonly needed conversions in the length category, required in engineering, science, and everyday life across different measurement systems.

Understanding mm and in

Millimetres and inches are both units of length. mm is part of the metric system, while in belongs to the imperial system. The exact conversion factor is 0.0393701 (25.4 in reverse).

When You Need This Conversion

This conversion is needed when working with international specifications, reading imperial measurements in a metric country, or using equipment calibrated in different units.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert mm to inches?

Divide mm by 25.4. 50 mm ÷ 25.4 = 1.969 inches. Exact conversion.

What is 100 mm in inches?

100 ÷ 25.4 = 3.937 inches. Quick estimate: ~4 inches.

Is 25 mm the same as 1 inch?

No. 25 mm = 0.984 in. 25.4 mm = exactly 1 inch.
