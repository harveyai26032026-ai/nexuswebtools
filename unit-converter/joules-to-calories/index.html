Background

Convert joules (J) to calories (cal) instantly. This is one of the most commonly needed conversions in the energy category, required in engineering, science, and everyday life across different measurement systems.

Understanding J and cal

Joules and calories are both units of energy. J is part of the metric system, while cal belongs to the imperial system. The exact conversion factor is 0.239006 (4.184 in reverse).

When You Need This Conversion

This conversion is needed when working with international specifications, reading imperial measurements in a metric country, or using equipment calibrated in different units.

Applications

Nutrition

4,184 J = 1 kcal (1 Calorie). Food energy labels use kcal or kJ.

Exercise

836,800 J = 200 kcal burned. Fitness trackers may use either unit.

Physics

1,000 J = 239 cal. Lab experiments report in joules.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert joules to calories?

Divide by 4.184. 1,000 J = 239 cal. For food Calories (kcal): divide by 4,184.

How many joules in a Calorie?

1 kcal (food Calorie) = 4,184 J = 4.184 kJ.

What's the difference between cal and Cal?

1 Calorie (kcal) = 1,000 calories. Food labels use Calories (capital C) = kilocalories.
