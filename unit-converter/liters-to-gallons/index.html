Background

Convert litres (L) to gallons (gal) instantly. This is one of the most commonly needed conversions in the volume category, required in engineering, science, and everyday life across different measurement systems.

Understanding L and gal

Litres and gallons are both units of volume. L is part of the metric system, while gal belongs to the imperial system. The exact conversion factor is 0.264172 (3.78541 in reverse).

When You Need This Conversion

This conversion is needed when working with international specifications, reading imperial measurements in a metric country, or using equipment calibrated in different units.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert litres to US gallons?

Multiply by 0.264172. 10 L = 2.64 US gal.

US vs imperial gallons?

US: 3.785 L. Imperial: 4.546 L. Imperial is 20% larger.

How many litres in a US gallon?

1 US gal = 3.78541 L.
