Background

Convert knots (kt) to miles per hour (mph) instantly. This is one of the most commonly needed conversions in the speed category, required in engineering, science, and everyday life across different measurement systems.

Understanding kt and mph

Knots and miles per hour are both units of speed. kt is part of the imperial system, while mph belongs to the metric system. The exact conversion factor is 1.15078 (0.868976 in reverse).

When You Need This Conversion

This conversion is needed when working with international specifications, reading metric measurements in a imperial country, or using equipment calibrated in different units.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert knots to mph?

Multiply by 1.15078. 100 knots = 115.1 mph.

What is 1 mph in knots?

1 mph = 0.868976 knots.

Why do boats and planes use knots?

1 knot = 1 nautical mile/hour. Nautical miles align with latitude degrees, simplifying navigation.
