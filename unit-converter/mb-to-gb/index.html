Background

Convert megabytes (MB) to gigabytes (GB) instantly. This is one of the most commonly needed conversions in the data category, required in engineering, science, and everyday life across different measurement systems.

Understanding MB and GB

Megabytes and gigabytes are both units of data. MB is part of the metric system, while GB belongs to the imperial system. The exact conversion factor is 0.001 (1000 in reverse).

When You Need This Conversion

This conversion is needed when working with international specifications, reading imperial measurements in a metric country, or using equipment calibrated in different units.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert MB to GB?

Decimal: divide by 1,000. Binary: divide by 1,024. 5,000 MB = 5 GB (decimal) or 4.88 GB (binary).

Why two different answers?

Storage makers use decimal (1 GB = 10⁹ B). OS uses binary (1 GB = 2³⁰ B). 7% difference.

How many MB in 1 GB?

1,000 MB (decimal) or 1,024 MB (binary).
